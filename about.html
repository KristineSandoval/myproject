ABOUT

Philosophy

PCNL believes in and advocates the developmental of man's vast potentials which, given the appropriate quality education and provided with a stimulating and nurturant environment, shall make him emerge as a self-actualized, fully productive and responsible member of society.

Vision

To be a distinct learning institution acknowledged far and wide for its innovative and flexible approaches in working with its students, stakeholders anf the community to create a more just and humane society and more sustainable economy.

Mission

In support of its philosophy, PCNL mandates itself to provide advances quality instruction to its clientele in education, the arts and humanities, the sciences, technology, business and other related professional and technical fields; promote the advancement of knowledge through research; and help improve the quality of community life through extension work.
